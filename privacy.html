Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

Thank you for playing Half Dead. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal identifying information (like your name or email) unless you provide it directly to us (e.g., via support email). However, we or our third-party partners may collect non-personal information automatically, including:

  • Device information (OS version, device model, language settings)
  • In-game activity (chapter progress, settings preferences)
  • Crash reports and performance data to improve the game

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Save your game progress locally on your device.
  • Analyze game crashes and performance issues.
  • Provide relevant in-app purchases (via Apple/Google payment services).

3. Third-Party Services

Our game utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These include:

  • Unity Technologies (Analytics & Crash Reporting) — Collects anonymous usage and crash data to help us improve the game. For more details, see Unity's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Services (Payments & Distribution) — Handles in-app purchases and app distribution. See Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Advertising & Tracking

Half Dead does not contain any advertisements. We do not use advertising SDKs, and we do not collect or share any data for advertising or tracking purposes. No advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID) are collected or accessed by our application.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Game progress and preferences are stored locally on your device using standard platform storage (PlayerPrefs). This data is deleted when you uninstall the app. We do not maintain any server-side user databases.

To request deletion of any data collected by third-party services, please contact us at the email address below, and we will assist you in exercising your rights.

6. Children's Privacy

Half Dead is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us with your data, and we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. Remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at yebappcontact@gmail.com.
