Frequently Asked Questions

  • How much of the game is free?
    Chapter 1 is completely free to play. You can unlock the full game (all chapters and endings) with a single one-time In-App Purchase.
  • Do I need an internet connection?
    No. Half Dead works fully offline after download.
  • How do I restore my purchase?
    Go to the game's main menu and tap "Restore Purchases." Your full game access will be restored if you previously purchased it with the same Apple ID.
  • The game is not working properly. What should I do?
    Try closing the app completely and reopening it. If the issue persists, make sure you have the latest version installed, then contact us via email.
  • Can I replay the game with different choices?
    Yes! You can restart chapters to explore different story paths and discover all the endings.
  • How do I change the language?
    The game follows your device's language setting. To change it, go to your device Settings > General > Language & Region.

In-App Purchase

Half Dead offers a single non-consumable In-App Purchase to unlock the full game. There are no subscriptions, no hidden fees, and no recurring charges. Once purchased, the full game is yours forever.
